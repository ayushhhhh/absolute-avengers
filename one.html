ABOUT US.

“Self-belief and hard work will always earn you success.”

These are words that we have always lived by. A subsidiary of an event management company based in New Delhi, Absolute Entertainment Pvt. Ltd. ABSOLUTE AVENGERS was founded on 26th February 2019 with the idea of bringing people together who share the same love and passion for cricket while promoting corporate cricket.

WHO WE ARE ?

We are a family of like-minded people from different worlds who share the same passion and love for cricket. A diverse group who otherwise would not have met, if not for their undying love for cricket. Over time our diversity became our greatest asset. Playing and bleeding together we have forged an unbreakable bond. The varied strengths every individual brings to the table gives our team a competitive edge over others.

MOTIVATION

We aim to bring corporates together to promote healthier competition through sportsmanship, discipline, teamwork and hard work. Our vision is that cricket can be played at any point of life regardless of your profession or age. We intend to increase mutual pride, respect and positivity among all teams and players from different paths of the world. To build a thriving, diverse and friendly community built on the mutual love for cricket.

GOALS

Already having won two corporate championships and being runner ups in four championships we have improved leaps and bounds and our goal is to become the best cricket team in Delhi NCR in the corporate sector. Furthermore, we wish to bring as many corporates as we can to build the community we have envisioned.
